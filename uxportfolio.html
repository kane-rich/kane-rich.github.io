UX Design and Research

Professional Diploma in UX Design Course - UX Design Institute

For the Professional Diploma in UX Design Course, we were tasked with following a case study project from inception through to completion. This took place in the shape of developing the flight booking process for the desktop website for fictional airline flyUX.

The project work on the course mirrored the entire Product / UX lifecycle: Research > Analysis > Design > Prototyping > Wireframing.

The projects I completed included:

  • Usability Testing
  • Affinity Diagram
  • Customer Journey Map
  • User Flow
  • Interaction Design
  • Mid-Fidelity Prototype
  • Wireframing
