Professional Diploma in UX Design Course - UX Design Institute

For the Professional Diploma in UX Design Course, we were tasked with following a case study project from inception through to completion. This took place in the shape of developing the booking process for a fictional airline's desktop website.

The projects I completed included:

  • Affinity Diagram
  • Customer Journey Map
  • User Flow
  • Interaction Design
  • Mid-Fidelity Prototype
  • Wireframing
